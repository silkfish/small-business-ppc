Small Business PPC: How to Compete With Bigger Budgets

Small businesses with $500-$2000/month PPC budgets are competing for the same clicks as enterprise advertisers spending $50,000/month. The math seems impossible. It is not.

You cannot win on bid amount. You can win on relevance, conversion rate, and tight targeting. Google's auction rewards quality, not just spend.

How small budgets win

  • Tight geographic targeting (3-5 mile radius around your service area, not entire metro)
  • High-intent keywords only (no broad informational terms)
  • Dayparting to your phone-answering hours
  • Single-product/service campaigns rather than catch-all
  • Aggressive negative keywords to eliminate waste
  • Landing pages tightly matched to ad copy

The Quality Score advantage

Google rewards relevance with lower CPCs. A small advertiser with a Quality Score of 9 can outbid an enterprise advertiser with a Quality Score of 5 — at half the cost per click. Relevance is the equalizer.
