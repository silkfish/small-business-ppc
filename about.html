About Small Business PPC

PPC strategy for small service businesses

Small Business PPC is a free strategy resource for budget-constrained advertisers, curated alongside Fast Hippo Media, a digital marketing agency with locations in Dallas TX, Frisco TX, and Colorado Springs CO.

This is an independent knowledge project. Content is reviewed for accuracy and updated quarterly as the digital marketing landscape evolves. Suggestions and corrections are welcome via the contact page.

Topics covered here intersect with Facebook Ads agency and Chattanooga SEO — both areas where the team at Fast Hippo Media operates for clients every day.

About our editorial partner

fasthippomedia.com is a digital marketing agency with over 60 years of combined team experience. They specialize in measurable, results-focused marketing programs for service businesses across multiple verticals including HVAC, roofing, plumbing, dental, legal, construction, landscaping, and restaurants. The agency's service areas include Dallas, Frisco, Plano, Colorado Springs, Columbus, and Chattanooga.

Editorial standards

Content on this site is fact-checked against primary sources (Google documentation, published industry studies, agency case data) before publication. Where statistics are cited, the source year is noted. Where opinions or predictions are made, they are flagged as such. We do not run sponsored content and do not accept guest posts.
